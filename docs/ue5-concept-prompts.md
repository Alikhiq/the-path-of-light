## The art bible (the shared look — already appended to each prompt)

THE PATH OF LIGHT — ART BIBLE (append verbatim to every prompt)

ENGINE / RENDER: rendered in Unreal Engine 5 styled as elevated PS1-retro low-poly — chunky faceted geometry, hard polygon edges, 128–256px dithered affine-mapped textures with visible texel grain, subtle vertex wobble on distant meshes, ordered Bayer dithering in shadow gradients, short draw distance dissolved by volumetric fog; yet composed cinematically with real depth, layered parallax silhouettes, and modern volumetric light.

CAMERA / LENS: virtual 35mm lens, gentle depth of field on the focal subject, slight low-angle reverence on architecture, rule-of-thirds or centered-symmetry compositions, mild vignette; first-person shots hold the horizon steady, third-person shots frame the player small against the world.

LIGHTING: two-source doctrine — warm oil-lamp pools (~2200K amber-gold) against cool high moonlight (~7000K blue); pools of gold light in oceans of navy shadow; god-rays through arches and smoke; every scene lit as night or deep dusk, never daylight.

PALETTE: deep navy-lapis night sky #0B1B3A, lapis blue mid-tones #1E3A8A, warm lamp gold #D9A84E, burnished gold highlights #F0C46B, teal accents on doors/tiles/domes #2B7A78, mudbrick ochre-brown #7A5C3E, near-black shadows #060B18.

نŪR LIGHT-BEING LOOK: all people are faceless نūر light-beings — warm golden glowing robed silhouettes, soft volumetric bloom at the edges, gentle inner gradient from gold core to amber rim, absolutely no eyes, no mouths, no facial features, no skin, no hair; robes read as simple low-poly Abbasid garments; important figures may instead appear as a floating gold calligraphic seal; NO sacred figures depicted, ever.

WORLD: period-accurate Abbasid Baghdad ~250 AH — mudbrick and fired-brick walls, horseshoe and pointed arches, squat domes, cylindrical minarets, wooden awnings and lattice mashrabiya, hanging oil lamps and standing braziers, carpets, scrolls, astrolabes, reed pens, clay jars, the Tigris with lashed-boat pontoon bridges; NO modern objects of any kind.

ATMOSPHERE / GRAIN: low navy volumetric fog hugging the ground and swallowing street ends, faint smoke haze above lamps, fine film grain over the whole frame, sparse drifting dust motes in light shafts, stars as single bright texels.

MOOD: reverent, hushed, contemplative, wonder without spectacle-noise — a holy quiet; the city feels alive but gentle.

NEGATIVE: no human faces, no depiction of God/prophets/Imams/sacred figures, no anime, no cartoon, no photorealistic people, no daylight, no modern items, no on-image text or UI unless the shot explicitly describes a game HUD element.
